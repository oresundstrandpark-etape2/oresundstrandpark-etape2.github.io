Introducing TradeBlotter

TradeBlotter allows you to track PnL and Risk on your positions, across depots. You can get notifications about trading holidays, important economic calendar events, earnings announcements, dividends, price targets from analysts and market news, for your positions. Realtime graphing of your PnL, and end-of-day PnL-mail/notification.

TradeBlotter can handle Equity, EFT, Bonds and Forex.

You can add trades manually or import them from a CSV-file. The import function is accessible via the web-portal.

You can select to get new notifications about your tickers, and/or daily mails about PnL.

Also you can get global market overview, inclusive swap rates matching duration of your bonds.

The calendar view and notification will help you to monitor important events in the markets, but only where you have positions or selects to watch or monitor.

Features

PnL

Your Profit and Loss is calculated every minute — for Today, Overall, Year-to-Date, Month-to-Date. Broken down to underlying (e.g. stock, forex, bond, etf) and forex contribution. Can be realtime graphed.

Risk

Your Risk is calculated hourly, notably delta, sigma and value-at-risk. Also sharpe ratio is calculated. This is done per position and per single underlying (e.g. stock, forex, bond, etf). Risk can be graphed versus PnL.

Trades

You can handle all trades across depots (e.g. bank, pension, e-trading) and get complete overview and easy trade reporting. You are notified about rating changes, earnings, dividend payments, price alerts and price targets from analysts, for your positions.

Market Overview

The global overview of key asian, euro and us indices and futures before opening is updated every minute showing changes to last session and hence sentiment. Swaprates are included matching any bonds you hold, plus Oil and Vol-of-Vol.

Calendar

You are notified about key economic events, and changes in keyfigures — and trading holidays, relating to your positions.

News

Web-sites and twitter channels are scanned continuously for news, relating to your positions. This will give notifications on your phone.

Flow

The 1 App to monitor your PnL, Risk, Calendar, News and Positions - global stocks, etfs, bonds, forex.
